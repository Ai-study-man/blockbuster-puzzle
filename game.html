Game Mechanics

Basic Matching

Connect three or more identical blocks horizontally or vertically to clear them from the board. Longer matches create more powerful special blocks.

Special Blocks

Create special blocks by matching 4+ pieces: rockets clear entire rows/columns, bombs explode in a 3x3 area, and rainbow blocks eliminate all blocks of one color.

Movie Themes

Each world features unique movie-themed elements with special mechanics like ice blocks that slide, fire blocks that spread, and mystery blocks with surprise effects.

Level Objectives

Score Challenges

Reach target scores by creating large matches and combos. Focus on special block combinations for maximum points.

Collection Tasks

Collect specific items by clearing blocks beneath them. Plan your moves to bring collectibles to the bottom of the board.

Obstacle Clearing

Remove barriers and blockers by matching adjacent pieces. Some obstacles require multiple hits or special blocks to clear.
