How to Play Blockbuster Puzzle

1

Match Same Blocks

Click or tap on adjacent blocks of the same type to select them. You need to match at least 3 identical blocks in a straight line (horizontal or vertical).

2

Create Combos

The more blocks you match at once, the bigger your combo! Matching 5+ blocks creates special explosive blocks that clear more of the board when used.

3

Complete Objectives

Each level has specific objectives like reaching a target score or clearing certain blocks. Watch the timer and plan your moves strategically!

Pro Tips & Strategies

Master the Basics

  • • Always look for the largest possible matches before making a move
  • • Plan your moves ahead - think about what blocks will fall after your match
  • • Focus on clearing blocks from the bottom to create cascading effects
  • • Save power-ups for challenging situations or when you need a high score

Advanced Techniques

  • • Create L-shaped or T-shaped matches for special blocks
  • • Combine two special blocks for maximum board clearing
  • • Use the movie theme hints to predict upcoming block patterns
  • • Master the timing of combo chains for massive score multipliers
